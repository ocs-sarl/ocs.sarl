About OCS

Office for Computer and Software (OCS), founded on January 9, 2023, in Kenitra, Morocco, is a technology company focused on delivering cutting-edge software and system solutions. Drawing inspiration from the vision of transforming future needs into present reality, OCS specializes in everything from custom software and AI solutions to data analysis and systems integration. It’s main product is Optivest. The company is built around a unique process that emphasizes collaboration with local university talent, aligning their training with European standards to deliver high-quality services at competitive costs. Creativity at OCS stems from deep technical know-how, an academic-industrial hybrid culture, and a strategic commitment to both national digital transformation and international expansion, particularly into African and English-speaking markets.
